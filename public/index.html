Proof of Concept

Knock the red cube off the island!

Movement
WASD or ↑←↓→ Move
Space Jump / Double Jump
Camera
P Toggle First/Third Person
Mouse Look Around (First-Person)
QE Rotate Left/Right (Third-Person)
RF Rotate Up/Down (Third-Person)
Right Click + Drag Rotate Camera (Third-Person)
Actions
